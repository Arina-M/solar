Is solar energy right for you

Is solar a good fit for your home?

Solar works best under the right conditions. It’s not the right choice for every home — and that’s okay.

Peak energy production relies on unobstructed access to sunlight. Ideal properties feature a south-facing roof (in the Northern Hemisphere) with a pitch between 15 and 40 degrees.

A professional site assessment ensures that environmental obstructions—such as mature foliage, chimneys, or adjacent structures—do not create significant shading losses, which can drastically reduce system efficiency.

Federal tax credit · ITC

Let The Government Pay For Nearly A Third Of Your Solar System

  • Valid through 2032 — the Inflation Reduction Act locked in the 30% rate for a decade.
  • Covers battery storage too — energy storage devices of 3 kWh or more qualify.
  • No income cap — available to all homeowners who owe federal taxes.
  • On a $15,000 system — that's $4,500 back on your tax return.
